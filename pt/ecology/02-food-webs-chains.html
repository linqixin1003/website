🔗 básico Concepts de comida Chains
comida chains are pathways para energy e nutrient transfer em ecosystems, showing "who eats whom" relationships. aves occupy diferente trophic levels em comida chains, de primary consumers para top predators.

🌱 Producers (primeiro Trophic Level)

Plants produce organic matter through photosynthesis, providing o energy foundation para o entire comida chain
  • Trees, shrubs, herbaceous plants
  • Algae, phytoplankton
  • Direct comida source para herbivorous aves

🦆 Primary Consumers (Second Trophic Level)

aves que feed directly em plants
  • Seed-eaters: Finches, pigeons, parrots
  • Fruit-eaters: Bulbuls, starlings, hornbills
  • Leaf-eaters: Geese, swans, alguns ducks
  • Nectar-feeders: Hummingbirds, sunbirds, honeyeaters

🐛 Secondary Consumers (Third Trophic Level)

aves que feed em insects e pequeno animals
  • Insectivorous aves: Swallows, flycatchers, wrens
  • Omnivorous aves: Crows, magpies, tits
  • pequeno fish-eaters: Kingfishers, cormorants, egrets

🦅 Tertiary Consumers (Fourth Trophic Level)

Raptors que feed em other aves e medium-sized animals
  • Diurnal raptors: Hawks, falcons, harriers
  • Nocturnal raptors: Owls, eagle-owls
  • grande fish-eaters: Pelicans, cormorants, sea eagles

🦴 Decomposers

aves que feed em carrion e participate em nutrient cycling
  • Vultures: Clean up grande animal carcasses
  • Corvids: Process various organic waste
  • alguns seabirds: Clean up dead fish em oceans
