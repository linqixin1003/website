🌸 ave Pollination Services

🌰 Seed Dispersal Services

aves are among o maioria importante seed dispersers, helping plants spread para novo habitats e maintaining genetic diversity em plant populations.

📦 Dispersal Types

Endozoochory

aves consume fruits e excrete seeds through their digestive tract

  • Seeds processed em o digestive tract
  • Improved germination rates
  • Relatively longo dispersal distances
Epizoochory

Seeds attach para feathers ou feet

  • Hooked ou sticky seeds
  • Medium dispersal distances
  • comum near água ou em wetlands
Scatter-hoarding

aves store seeds, alguns remain uneaten

  • Jays storing acorns
  • Nutcrackers storing pine seeds
  • Improved seed survival rates

🍓 Fruit-ave Relationships

  • Fruit colors match Visão das Aves
  • Fruit tamanho suitable para ave mouthparts
  • Nutritional content meets ave requirements
  • Ripening tempo synchronized com ave activity
  • Fruit display position facilitates ave alimentação

📊 Dispersal Effectiveness
