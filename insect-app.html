About InsectAiSnap

InsectAiSnap will be developed in partnership with entomologists, ecologists, and conservation biologists to create the most comprehensive insect identification and education platform available.

Our goal is to foster appreciation for insect diversity while providing practical tools for researchers, educators, gardeners, and nature enthusiasts to understand and protect these vital creatures.
