Safe Restraint Techniques
Proper restraint is essential for examination and treatment while minimizing stress and injury risk. Birds are fragile and can be easily injured by improper handling ü§≤.
Towel Restraint Method:
‚Ä¢ Use a small, soft towel
‚Ä¢ Approach calmly from behind
‚Ä¢ Gently drape towel over bird
‚Ä¢ Support head and neck carefully
‚Ä¢ Avoid pressure on chest/abdomen
‚Ä¢ Keep restraint time minimal
Safe Handling Guidelines:
‚Ä¢ Never grab by wings, tail, or legs
‚Ä¢ Support the body weight properly
‚Ä¢ Keep head controlled but not compressed
‚Ä¢ Watch for signs of respiratory distress
‚Ä¢ Release immediately if bird struggles excessively
‚Ä¢ Have someone assist if possible

‚ö†Ô∏è Restraint Warnings

  • Panic response: Can cause self-injury
