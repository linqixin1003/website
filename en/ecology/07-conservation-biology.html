üìä Conservation Status Assessment

Scientific assessment of bird conservation status is the foundation for establishing conservation priorities.

üî¥ IUCN Red List Categories

  • Extinct (EX): Species completely disappeared
  • Extinct in the Wild (EW): Only captive individuals remain
  • Critically Endangered (CR): Facing extremely high extinction risk
  • Endangered (EN): Facing very high extinction risk
  • Vulnerable (VU): Facing high extinction risk
  • Near Threatened (NT): Close to qualifying for a threatened status
  • Least Concern (LC): Not facing extinction threat
