Spring: The Vibrant Breeding Season
Spring is one of the most exciting seasons for birdwatching. As temperatures rise and daylight hours extend, birds enter breeding season with frequent activity and constant singingüå∏. This is the best time to observe courtship behaviors, nest-building activities, and territorial disputes.
Spring migration is another birdwatching highlight. Coastal areas, mountain passes, and river valleys are ideal locations for observing migratory birds.

Between 4-8 AM is when birds are most active, with songs at their most frequent. Pay attention to trees with new leaves, as many insectivorous birds will forage there.

Fall migration is typically more spectacular and prolonged than spring migration. Birds forage frequently to store energy for long-distance flightsüçÇ. The season of ripening fruits also attracts numerous frugivorous birds, providing excellent observation opportunities for birdwatchers.

Mastering seasonal birdwatching patterns requires long-term observation and recording.
