When Birds Are Considered Senior:

• Medium birds (cockatiels, conures): 10-15 years

Physical Signs of Aging:
• Decreased activity and energy levels
• Changes in feather quality and color
• Weight loss or difficulty maintaining weight
• Reduced grip strength and balance
• Cloudy or dim eyes
• Slower movement and reaction times
• Changes in sleep patterns
Behavioral Changes in Senior Birds:
• Increased sleeping and resting
• Reduced vocalizations
• Less interest in toys and activities
• Changes in social interactions
• Increased irritability or sensitivity
• Preference for familiar routines
• Difficulty adapting to changes
