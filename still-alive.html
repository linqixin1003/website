Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Still Alive? ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data We Collect

  • Device Information: We collect device identifiers to ensure the app functions correctly on your specific device.
  • Check-in Data: We store your check-in history locally on your device and sync status with our secure server.
  • Emergency Contacts: Information about your emergency contacts (name, email, phone) is stored securely to send alerts when needed.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely for the purpose of providing the safety check-in service:

  • To monitor your check-in status.
  • To send notifications to you (reminders).
  • To send emergency alerts to your contacts if you fail to check in.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your personal information is encrypted and we do not sell your data to third parties.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at lingjuetech@gmail.com
